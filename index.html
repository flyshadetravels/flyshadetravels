Munnar, Backwaters & Kochi

Kochi → Munnar → Alappuzha → Kochi
  • Munnar sightseeing, tea estates & elephant safari
  • Alappuzha backwater cruise, shikara ride & beach
  • Fort Kochi heritage & Chinese fishing nets
View day-by-day +
Day 1 — Into the hillsPick-up from Kochi and a scenic climb to Munnar past Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls, with stops at Second Mile and Pipeline viewpoints. Evening free to wander the town and spice markets.
Day 2 — Munnar sightseeingA full day among the tea estates — Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, Rose Garden, Munnar Village viewpoint, Photo Point, elephant safari, Mattupetty tea factory, Mattupetty Dam & boating, and Echo Point.
Day 3 — Backwaters & AlappuzhaDrive to Alappuzha for a leisurely backwater cruise / shikara ride and an evening at Alappuzha beach, then check in to your hotel.
Day 4 — Fort Kochi & farewellDrive to Kochi to explore Fort Kochi beach, the Water Metro, Chinese fishing nets, Jew Town, Mattancherry Palace, the Synagogue and the Folklore Museum, with a stop at Lulu Mall before your transfer to Kochi airport / station.
