Grand Kerala Discovery

Kochi → Munnar → Thekkady → Alappuzha → Varkala → Kovalam → Trivandrum
  • Munnar hills, Thekkady wildlife & backwaters
  • Varkala cliff, black-sand beach & kayaking
  • Kovalam beaches, temples & Arabian Sea sunsets
View day-by-day +
Day 1 — Into the hillsPick-up from Kochi and a scenic climb to Munnar past Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls, with stops at Second Mile and Pipeline viewpoints. Evening free to wander the town and spice markets.
Day 2 — Munnar sightseeingA full day among the tea estates — Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, Rose Garden, Munnar Village viewpoint, Photo Point, elephant safari, Mattupetty tea factory, Mattupetty Dam & boating, and Echo Point. Also a chocolate factory, handloom unit and spice garden visit, an ayurvedic massage, and an evening cultural programme.
Day 3 — Thekkady & PeriyarDrive to Thekkady to visit Periyar National Park — a boat ride on the lake and a jeep safari through the reserve.
Day 4 — Backwaters & AlappuzhaDrive to Alappuzha for a leisurely backwater cruise / shikara ride and an evening at Alappuzha beach, then check in to your hotel.
Day 5 — On to VarkalaDrive towards Varkala with a stop at the Jatayu Earth's Center, then settle in by Varkala's famous cliff and beach.
Day 6 — VarkalaA relaxed day around Varkala — the black-sand beach, the cliff and beach, and mangrove backwater kayaking.
Day 7 — Towards Trivandrum & KovalamDrive south via Kappil beach and lake, Anjengo fort and lighthouse, the Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Shankumugham beach, then check in near Kovalam for the evening.
Day 8 — Beach & farewellA morning at Kovalam beach, some local-market shopping and a stop at Lulu Mall, then transfer to Trivandrum airport / station.
